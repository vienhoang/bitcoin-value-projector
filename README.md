# Bitcoin Value Projector

A free, privacy-first bitcoin investment calculator and HODL simulator. Project your bitcoin investments with customizable growth scenarios. All calculations run directly in your browser with zero data collection.

**Live:** [bitcoin-value-projector.pages.dev](https://bitcoin-value-projector.pages.dev)

## 🚀 Features

- **Bitcoin Stack Calculator** - Track your current holdings and their value in real-time.
- **Investment Projections** - Forecast your bitcoin portfolio value across multiple years.
- **Multiple CAGR Scenarios** - Conservative (15%), Moderate (25%), or Bullish (40%) growth scenarios.
- **Custom Growth Rates** - Set your own annual compound growth rate for personalized projections.
- **Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)** - Simulate regular monthly bitcoin purchases.
- **Interactive Visualization** - Beautiful charts showing projected vs. actual investment growth.
- **Timeline View** - Year-by-year breakdown of portfolio value and multiples.
- **ROI Tracking** - Monitor your current profit/loss and return on investment.
- **Top Supporters** - Honor roll of bitcoin donors with sortable Name and Amount columns.
- **100% Private** - All calculations run locally, no data is sent to any server.

## 📊 Use Cases

- **HODL Planning** - See potential long-term gains from your bitcoin holdings.
- **DCA Strategy** - Evaluate the impact of regular monthly bitcoin purchases.
- **Scenario Analysis** - Compare conservative vs. bullish growth projections.
- **Investment Goals** - Calculate what price bitcoin needs to reach your financial targets.
- **Decision Making** - Model different purchase strategies and timeframes.

## 📖 How to Use

### 1. **Your Bitcoin Stack**
   - **Bitcoin Amount:** Enter how much bitcoin you own
   - **Average Purchase Price:** Enter your cost basis per BTC
   - **Current Bitcoin Price:** Enter today's BTC/USD price
   - **Total Amount Invested:** Auto-calculated from amount × purchase price

### 2. **Projection Settings**
   - Choose from preset scenarios or enter a custom CAGR percentage
   - Select your target projection year (2026–2050)
   - Optionally add monthly DCA purchases to simulate regular buys
   - Click "Calculate Projection"

### 3. **Results**
   - **Current Value:** Your bitcoin's value at today's price
   - **Profit/Loss:** Total gain or loss in USD
   - **ROI So Far:** Return on investment percentage
   - **Projection:** Estimated value at your target year
   - **Interactive Chart:** Visual representation of growth over time
   - **Timeline:** Year-by-year breakdown with multiples
